Each point on the path is the residual stream after one more + (Euler step). Logit lens: what the head would pick if the path stopped and fired here. J-lens: the state transported through the model's average remaining flow (Jℓ = E[∂hfinal/∂hℓ]) before decoding — what it's disposed to make the model say later, the paper's window into silent thoughts. Method: Anthropic, “Verbalizable Representations Form a Global Workspace in Language Models”; lens files fitted & hosted by Neuronpedia.

Raw q/k/v projection outputs — a content view (pre-RoPE, and pre-QK-norm on Qwen3), not the rotated vectors the attention dot product sees. The k and v rows are what this token publishes to the k/v stream at this layer; q is the question it asks. Arc weights are max over heads; position 0 (the attention sink) is omitted, so weights don't sum to 1.
